Angry Birds Facts

  • Angry Birds was released in December 2009.
  • The game was created by a Finnish company called Rovio Entertainment.
  • The birds in the game were inspired by wingless birds from a sketch created by a designer at Rovio.
  • Angry Birds is one of the most downloaded mobile games of all time.
  • The game has spawned multiple sequels and spin-offs.
  • Angry Birds was turned into a movie in 2016.
  • The birds and pigs in the game are named after various types of meat.
  • The game has been downloaded over 3 billion times.
  • Angry Birds was initially released only for iOS, but it later became available on other platforms.
  • Rovio was on the brink of bankruptcy before Angry Birds became a hit.
